Jessica Blue

Visual Artist & Designer

Poppies (2010)

acrylic painting on canvas (24" x 36"). This painting was done for a gift exchange a few years ago, so I sadly no longer have the original. Definitely one of my favourites.

Hex Skull (2015)

ink sketch on paper (in sketchbook). This was done as an effort to do more design-type drawings. A friend of mine recently just got this tattooed on his thigh.

Grapefruits (2012)

acrylic painting on canvas (36" x 36"). A still life painting done for art school. This is one of three paintings done for the series and all three are currently hanging in my house.

Caryatids Porch (2015)

ink sketch on paper (in sketchbook). I recently returned from a trip to Europe and I spent a full month in Greece. This drawing was done 'en plein air' while visiting the Acropolis in Athens

Voyeurism (2014)

oil painting on canvas (48" x 52"). Another product of art school. This one was a response to a Windows/relection theme. I experimented with different oil mediums and ways of representing "real" space versus reflected space.

Highland Sheep (2015)

ink sketch on paper (in sketchbook). This was also done on my trip last year, although I used a reference photo this time. I spent four days hiking in the highlands of Scotland and the sheep were my lovely companions.

Segal Logo (2015)

digital image created on Adobe Illustrator. I was hired by the Segal research lab at UVic this past fall to create a new logo for their website and other promotional materials. The sleek and simple design came together very well.

Vertical Madness Logo (2012)

digital image created on Adobe Photoshop. This logo was done a few years back for a friend who makes skydiving rigs and clothing, as an emblem to appear on his creations. The design incorporates the company initials with a parachute.

Dubrovnik (2015)

ink sketch on paper (in sketchbook). This image includes progress shots as well as the final drawing. Also done from a reference photo from my travels. I loved the intricate roofs in old town Dubrovnik and wanted to capture that intricacy in my line drawing.

about me

I am a visual artist and graphic designer from Victoria, British Columbia in Canada

I enjoy making the world a more beautiful place through creativity and colour. When I'm not kicking it in my art corner, you'll find me adventuring in the great outdoors.
